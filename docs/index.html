DNS Record Recommendations

Our hosted email infrastructure is continually evolving. As a result, IP addresses will change as part of improving the service. To ensure the best connectivity, use mx[1-2].emailsrvr.com in DNS and secure.emailsrvr.com on sending software. Hard-coding IP addresses will lead to future delivery problems for your domain.
